Biography

My name is Isioma Pamela Talabi and I currently live in Abuja, Nigeria. I was born in Lagos Nigeria but grew up mostly in the Federal Capital Territory.

Early Years

My formative years were spent going to an Anglican Nursery and Primary School which was situated next to a bookshop. My momma is an avid reader and so, I became one too. Having a bookshop next to my school also helped my reading journey. By the time I was 10 years old, I had read a lot of famours writings by African Authors such as Chinua Achebe, Elechi Amadi, Flora Nwapa and Ngugi wa Thiong'o. I went on to study Economics and Development Studies in a private university in Nigeria where I graduated with a First Class Honours. After my graduation, I had a brief stint working as an investment banker in a financial institution. Soon after, I switched careers to the international development sector because I wanted to use my skills to help people around the world. I have now amassed over 10 years of working experience in the International Development sector with a brief stint in Investment Banking. Currently, I work as the Global Head of Knowledge Management & Learning at Save the Children International, where I lead organisational learning efforts and processes in over 120 countries. In 2017, I received a Commonwealth Scholarship Award to study International Development in the United Kingdoms after which I returned to Nigeria where I worked in the Office of the Vice President of the Federal Republic of Nigeria as a Technical Assistant on SGBV Response. I have also worked with organisations such as PwC and Palladium in the areas of social justice and rural development. In my free time, I enjoy watching movies and trying out new cuisines with my husband and son.

Joining Alt School: My Reasons & Goals

I joined Alt School Africa in 2024 because I have always had a great love for creating new and beautiful things. I am fascinated by codes and beyond that, I love websites that look good and obey the principles of good design. I am also at the point where I would like to pivot my career into tech and gain a new set of skills.

My Goals at Alt School

I intend to accomplish the following goals during my 1 year course at Alt School:

  1. Develop proficiency in website programming languages such as HTML, CSS, Javascript and React to build real-life businesses.
  2. Build a network of relationship with other developers.
  3. Develop a solid understanding of how to maintain workflows on GitHub.
  4. Improve my technical skills and interpersonal skills.
  5. Learn how to teach Front End Development concepts and programming languages.

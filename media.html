Section 2: Faith & My Beautiful Catholic Life

I am in a loving relationship with Jesus Christ and I express this relationship through living out my Catholic faith.
Everyday, I am in awe of how intentional God is and how he uses the Blessed Virgin Mary and all the angels and saints of God to continuously draw us into His presence. In this section I outline visual and audio displays of some of the things that bring joy to my faith.



Some songs I love:

Title: Iruele Maria by Obioha Ogbonna

Description:
Composed by one of the best Nigerian, Catholic composers, this beautiful rendition is the beloved 'Hail Mary' prayer in the Etsako language of Edo state, Nigeria. One of my best songs of 2024!

Title: Tewo Gbegbowa

Description:
A beautiful song imploring God to accept the gifts we offer to Him. Sung by the St. Albert Choir of UNIBEN.



Video I love currently:

This powerful message from Monsignor James Shea is a moving one about how we humans are called to hunger and seek God to fill the void in our life.
He journeys from Genesis to Jesus's death on the cross and finally to the book of Revealation
describing to us God's intentionality to set us free from sin and to call us into a vibrant life in Him.
